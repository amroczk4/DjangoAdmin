    Tutorial

    How To Play:


    You have 6 tries to guess the dish


    After each guess, you will get the main ingredient, distance & direction from the target country of origin, 3 hints on the taste profile and hint based on calorie count


    Your Guessed Dish
    Country, Distance & Direction
    Taste Profile 1
    Taste Profile 2
    Taste Profile 3
    Calories
    Main Ingredient

    Country, Distance & Direction will be provided in one circle

    Taste profiles will be hinted through three different circles. Although sometimes some dishes may not have more than 2 taste profiles according to our database

    Calorie hint is based of how far your guess's calorie is different from the answer's calorie

    Provides the main ingredient of your guessed dish

    Taste profiles and main ingredient is color coded to have:

    • Green to be correct
    • Yellow to be close
    • red to be wrong
