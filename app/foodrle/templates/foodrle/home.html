How To Play:


You have 6 tries to guess the dish.


After each guess, you will get the main ingredient, distance & direction from the target country of origin, 3 hints on the taste profile and hint based on calorie count.


Your Guessed Dish
Country, Distance & Direction
Taste Profile 1
Taste Profile 2
Taste Profile 3
Calories
Main Ingredient

Country, Distance & Direction will be provided in one circle.

Taste profiles will be hinted through three different circles. Although some dishes may be described with only two taste qualities.

Calorie hint is based on how far the calorie count of the guessed dish is from the answer's calorie count.

Provides the main ingredient of the guessed dish.

Taste profiles and main ingredient are color coded as follows:

  • Green to be correct
  • Yellow to be close (only for main ingredient)
  • Red to be wrong
